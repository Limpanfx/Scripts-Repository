# Bear – Player Magnet

Simple GUI-based script for Bear that teleports all other players to you and keeps them stuck to your character with collisions disabled.
Practically instant win bear script.

## Features

- Draggable GUI with title bar and close button.  
- Single toggle button to activate/deactivate the magnet.  
- Teleports all other players’ HumanoidRootParts to your own on a loop.  
- Disables collisions on other players while active and restores them when off.  
- Status label showing ACTIVE / INACTIVE.  
- RightControl to quickly hide/show the UI.
